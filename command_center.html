Numöbius v5.2 — Quick Start Guide
64-Dimensional Reversible Solver · R.U.B.I.C. Framework · 840-Clock Phase System · SMS Alerts · Market Sweep · Chinese Markets

1 Getting Started
1
Pick a Ticker
Type a symbol in the search bar (e.g. AAPL, BTC-USD) or click one from the Watchlist on the left. Toggle between Equities and Crypto mode — crypto uses shorter timeframes and faster indicators.
2
Run Analysis
Click the ▶ Analyze button. The 64D solver launches hundreds of parallel instances, converges on an energy minimum, and passes the result through all 14 R.U.B.I.C. framework systems. Takes 1–3 seconds. The KPI bar, chart, and all right-side tabs update with results.
3
Read Your Signal
The Signal tab shows: direction (BUY / SELL / WAIT), confidence %, entry price, stop loss (2×ATR), and two reward targets (1.5R, 3R). Below that, the framework metrics show Ladder topology, Triality Synergy, Fusion state, and digital root phase. The next-day forecast gives predicted OHLC.
4
Set Up the Scheduler
Go to the Schedule tab. Set an interval (e.g. 15 min for crypto, 1 hr for equities), enter your tickers comma-separated, and click ▶ Start. Numöbius auto-cycles through each ticker, logs predictions, and scores them against live prices. Keep the tab open.
5
Monitor Accuracy
The Accuracy tab tracks live performance: direction win rate, average price error, per-ticker breakdowns, and P&L. Use this to calibrate confidence in the signals. The Log tab stores every prediction as exportable .jsonl data.

3 Reading Signals
Direction is computed from the solver state, RSI, price momentum, and regime coherence. The 840-clock phase gates what signals can fire: V-phase blocks SELL (trend mode), S−-phase blocks BUY (reversal mode), S+ allows both.
Confidence combines 7 components: CNLT forcing (20%), uncertainty (15%), phase alignment (15%), convergence (15%), triality synergy (15%), Flower-of-Life entropy (10%), and norm stability (10%). Multipliers apply for regime (BULL/BEAR +15/10%), E8 alignment (+8%), and 7-crowding singularity (+20%). A norm deviation circuit breaker cuts confidence by 40% if the solver state is unstable.
Regime coherence: counter-regime signals (e.g. SELL in BULL) require confirming price momentum. Without it, the signal is forced to WAIT. With momentum support, counter-regime signals receive a 25% confidence penalty and STRONG signals are downgraded.
Predictions show a multi-day price trajectory. The curve is modulated by ladder orientation, FoL entropy (low entropy = less noise), fusion absorption (dampens volatility at zero-divisor events), and triality synergy (scales directional bias).
4 R.U.B.I.C. Framework — 14 Systems
The solver state passes through 14 interconnected mathematical systems before producing a signal. These appear in the Signal tab under "𝒩₄ Ladder · Hypercomplex Dynamics".
Fano Plane Octonions
8D non-associative algebra via 7 directed triples. Used as a solver gate every 64 steps — richer state mixing than XOR.
Cayley-Dickson Sedenions
16D algebra via doubling: (a,b)(c,d) = (ac−d̄b, da+bc̄). Introduces zero-divisors for fusion detection.
Numöbius Ladder 𝒩₄
Double-rim Möbius topology: 4 bottom (octonionic) + 4 top (sedenionic) nodes. Portal-up on strong forcing, collapse-down on weak.
7-Crowding Singularity
When collapse maps converge to node 7 (the absorption point), a volatility crush signal fires with +20% confidence boost.
REOFFSET Invariant
Each collapse flips orientation 180° on the Möbius strip. Used as a regime inversion signal.
Zero-Divisor Fusion
Detects sedenionic pairs xy≈0 via variance collapse + structural triple matching. High absorption dampens prediction volatility.
K-Probe 3-Lens
Physical (280 events) → Signal (σ>25%) → Crypto (33 Bresenham). Three observation layers measuring different market scales.
840-Clock Sync
4-system synchronization: E8 mod 30, triality mod 3, digital root 1→4→7, mod 8. Phase determines signal gating.
Digital Root (1,4,7)
Cycling marker: 1=initiation, 4=confirmation, 7=resolution. At resolution, WAIT signals lean into price momentum.
Flower of Life RCA
19-node reversible cellular automaton on hex lattice. Shannon entropy measures state structure — low entropy = less prediction noise.
Triality Synergy
k=3 probe information recovery vs k=2 baseline. Target: 120.5%. Scales directional bias in predictions.
Norm Preservation
Tracks 8D octonion sub-norm deviation. >30% deviation triggers circuit breaker (confidence ×0.6).
E8 Root Alignment
Every 30th tick aligns with E8 Coxeter group. Gives +8% confidence boost and 15% noise reduction on predictions.
Jones Polynomial
Tracks braiding invariant over 100-step orbits. Diagnostic metric for topological complexity of the solver path.
5 The 840-Clock
The 840-clock partitions each analysis cycle into three phases of 280 ticks each. 840 = lcm(3,5,7,8) — it synchronizes triality (mod 3), digital roots (mod 9→1,4,7), E8 alignment (mod 30), and octonionic structure (mod 8).
V (Vector, ticks 0–279): Trend-following mode. SELL signals are blocked. Best for momentum entries. S+ (Spinor+, 280–559): Both directions allowed. Highest signal diversity. S− (Spinor−, 560–839): Reversal mode. BUY signals are blocked. Best for mean-reversion or short entries.
The KPI bar shows the current tick count and phase. The Strategy tab tracks which phases produce winning signals historically for your data.
6 Scheduler & Automation
The Schedule tab lets you run unattended analysis. Set the interval (5 min to 24 hr), enter comma-separated tickers, and click Start. Each cycle: fetches latest prices → runs full 64D analysis → logs predictions → scores prior predictions against actual prices → updates accuracy metrics.
Prediction Tracker shows each scheduled prediction alongside the live price. Green checkmark = correct direction, red X = missed. Use this as the ground truth for evaluating signal quality.
Tips: For crypto, use 5–15 min intervals (markets move fast). For equities, 30 min–1 hr is sufficient. Keep the browser tab open — the scheduler runs client-side. Enable Auto-Log in Solver settings to save every prediction to the log.

Phase (V/S+/S−)840-clock phase. V=trend (no SELL), S+=momentum (any signal), S−=reversal (no BUY).
Ladder NodePosition on the 𝒩₄ Möbius ladder. Bottom nodes (4,7,10,13) = octonionic layer. Top (16,19,22,25) = sedenionic.
